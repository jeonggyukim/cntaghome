Computational & Theoretical Astrophysics Group

About

We are Computational and Theoretical Astrophysics Group (CnTAG) of the Astronomy Program in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at Seoul National University, Korea. The CnTAG is a participating member of the Center for the Exploration of the Origin of the Universe (CEOU).

Research

Our research agenda encompasses a diversity of astrophysical phenomena that involve gas dynamical processes. Examples include formation of giant molecular clouds, barred and/or spiral galaxies and star formation therein, regulation of star formation rate in a galaxy, and dynamics and instability of photoionized gas around young massive stars.
